Welcome to Our Club

Kyiv Dice & Board is a community of board game enthusiasts who gather every week to play, learn, and share their love of tabletop games. Whether you enjoy strategy, cooperation, or simply rolling dice, there is a place for you at our table.

We opened our doors in 2023 and have grown into one of Kyiv’s most welcoming game spaces. Our library holds over 200 board games, and our café serves great coffee and snacks while you play.

🎲 Game Nights

Join us every Friday and Saturday for open game nights. Bring your own game or pick one from our library—all skill levels are welcome.
