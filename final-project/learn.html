Quick Rule Cards

Hover over each card to reveal a quick summary of the game rules. These cards cover the most requested games at our club.

🎲 Catan
Collect resources, build roads & settlements, and trade with other players. First to 10 victory points wins!
🌎 Pandemic
Work together as a team to cure 4 diseases before they spread across the globe. Cooperation is key!
🔍 Codenames
Two teams compete to find their agents using one-word clues. Avoid the assassin at all costs!
🛫 Ticket to Ride
Collect train cards and claim railway routes across the map. Complete your destination tickets for bonus points.
🦅 Wingspan
Attract birds to your nature reserve. Combine food, eggs, and special powers to build the best habitat.
🃏 Dixit
Use beautiful illustrated cards and your imagination. Give a clue that’s not too easy and not too hard!
♛ Chess
The classic strategy game. Move your pieces to checkmate the opponent’s king. Simple rules, endless depth.
💫 Exploding Kittens
Draw cards and try not to draw an Exploding Kitten. Use action cards to skip, peek, or defuse!

Why Board Games Matter

We don’t stop playing because we grow old; we grow old because we stop playing.
— George Bernard Shaw

Board games help develop critical thinking, patience, and social skills. Studies show that playing tabletop games can reduce stress by up to 20% and improve memory and cognitive function.

Game Night Etiquette

  1. Read the rules before your first game — or ask a host for help.
  2. Be respectful: winning & losing are both part of the fun.
  3. Keep food & drinks away from the game components — use the café tables.
  4. Return all pieces to the box when you’re done – we appreciate it!
  5. Temperature in the club is kept at 22°C for comfort.
