Therapeutic Garden Opens at Kyiv’s Expocenter

A new therapeutic garden has opened at the Expocenter of Ukraine (VDNG) in Kyiv, offering nature-based rehabilitation for veterans and civilians. The project features sensory paths, raised beds, and guided relaxation sessions.
